About Us

Beans 'n Creams

Taste and See!!! Beans 'n Creams offers a unique coffee environment unlike any other in SOUTH KOREA. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to relax, have a cup of coffee in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body. We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Safehouse Coffee made by our professionally trained chefs. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our beans are obtained naturally and our coffee is made with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. We look forward for your orders form Beans 'n Creams!!!
